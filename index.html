About me

My name is Ana Rita, I'm a Portuguese astrophysicist and PhD candidate.
My host institutions are the University of Porto, the Institute of Astrophysics and Space Sciences (CAUP), and the Observatory of Geneva. I'm happiest when I'm learning, whatever the subject may be: astronomy, languages, crafts, you name it.

Learn more

My research

The focus of my research is exoplanet atmospheres, which I probe using high-resolution spectroscopy. At the moment, I'm working with data from ESPRESSO (VLT, Paranal) and NIRPS (3.6m, La Silla).

Science Communication

I dedicate a large portion of my time to science communication and outreach because I was once a little girl going to outreach events and having my mind blown.
How could I not be a part of that now?
